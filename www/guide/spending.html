Spending

The Spending view shows where your money goes, and it keeps the two pots apart. They are different accounts in different currencies, so Daimond shows them as two tracks and never adds them together.

Two pots, kept apart

  • Inference. The model calls themselves, billed to your own provider key. Daimond prices and records them on this device, per turn, so you can see the cost of your own key spend without any of it passing through us.
  • Credits. The prepaid balance the gateway spends on the few things that leave the browser: fetching a page, syncing or sending mail, and cross-device sync.

Opening it

Open the Spending view from the spend meter in the top bar, or from a link in the Credits view. It shows each pot with a headline figure, a daily graph, a breakdown by category, and a plain table of movements, so a charge is never a mystery.
